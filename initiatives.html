Community Initiatives

Smaller community-based programs are also making a difference. Organizations host e-waste collection events and promote recycling, helping communities dispose of electronics responsibly.

Reducing E-Waste Through Repair Initiatives

Programs that encourage repairing rather than replacing electronics help reduce e-waste. Repair cafes and tech-for-good initiatives are growing in popularity worldwide.
